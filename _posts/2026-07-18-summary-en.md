## [Doubao Phone abandons GUI simulation, switches to MCP, boosts shipment to hundreds of thousands](https://www.latepost.com/news/dj_detail?id=3648) ⭐️ 9.0/10

ByteDance's Doubao Phone has abandoned the GUI-based screen reading and simulated click strategy for controlling third-party apps. Instead, it now requires these apps to offer MCP services for integration, and has increased production preparation from 30,000 to hundreds of thousands of units. This shift signals a major change in how AI phone assistants interact with super apps, moving from adversarial screen scraping to cooperative API-based integration. It could redefine the power dynamics between device makers, app developers, and AI platforms, potentially leading to more stable and secure AI automation on smartphones. The initial GUI approach faced immediate blocks from major apps like WeChat and Taobao, forcing ByteDance to downscale. The new MCP-based approach requires app developers to voluntarily expose data and controls, which is similar to Apple's App Intents framework. The separate AI service filing obtained on July 15 may facilitate future cooperation with more manufacturers.
> **Reason**: 晚点独家报道豆包手机放弃GUI模拟点击策略，转向MCP服务合作，备货量从3万提升至数十万台，体现了AI手机助手与超级应用合作模式的重要变化，对行业格局有潜在影响。

## [Kimi K3: 2.8 Trillion Parameter Open Model Surpasses GPT-5 and Claude Opus](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Jul/16/kimi-k3/) ⭐️ 8.0/10

Moonshot AI announced Kimi K3, an open-weights model with 2.8 trillion parameters, claiming it surpasses GPT-5 and Claude Opus on multiple benchmarks. The weights will be released by July 27, 2026. Kimi K3 is the first open model to exceed 2 trillion parameters, signaling that open-source AI can compete with proprietary frontier models. Its pricing at $3/$15 per million tokens positions it as a premium product, challenging the cost dynamics of the AI market. The model uses 2.8 trillion parameters, supports a 1M context window, and achieves an Elo of 1547 on Artificial Analysis' long-horizon knowledge work evaluation. However, it lags behind Claude Fable 5 and GPT-5.6 Sol on some benchmarks.

## [EU AI Act OpenRAG: 933 structured chunks with BGE-M3 embeddings](https://www.reddit.com/r/MachineLearning/comments/1uytlac/eu_ai_act_openrag_933_legally_structured_chunks/) ⭐️ 8.0/10

The author released EU AI Act OpenRAG, a corpus that chunks Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 by legal structure (933 chunks) and includes BGE-M3 embeddings stored in a SQLite database. This dataset bridges legal domain expertise and RAG by using structural chunking, achieving higher recall than sliding windows, which is critical for legal NLP and AI Act compliance systems. The corpus includes exact EUR-Lex links, Article 113 application-date metadata, and separate storage for direct textual classification and broad regulatory associations. Retrieval evaluation shows scenario article recall@20 of 0.541 (structural) vs 0.449 (baseline).
> **Reason**: 作者发布了专门针对EU AI Act的RAG语料库，包含结构化分块和嵌入，技术细节丰富，对legal-NLP研究有价值。Reddit社区有讨论。

## [Huawei Ascend 950 SuperPoD debuts, claims 6.7x performance over Nvidia](https://www.ithome.com/0/978/019.htm) ⭐️ 8.0/10

Huawei unveiled the Ascend 950 SuperPoD (Atlas 950) at WAIC 2026, offering 1 EFLOPS FP8 and 2 EFLOPS FP4 compute, with 1024 NPU cards connected via the UnifiedBus protocol. It is claimed to deliver 6.7 times the performance of comparable Nvidia systems. This marks a major milestone for domestic AI chips in China, challenging Nvidia's dominance in high-performance AI compute. The Ascend 950 SuperPoD could reshape the AI infrastructure landscape for Chinese enterprises and government clients seeking alternatives amid export restrictions. The system features 256 TB unified global memory, TB-level NPU interconnect bandwidth, and 3μs ultra-low RTT latency. The earlier Ascend 384 SuperPoD has already seen commercial deployment in over 750 units across industries including internet, telecom, finance, and healthcare.
> **Reason**: 华为昇腾950超节点首次公开亮相，提供详细技术参数和行业部署数据，是国产AI算力的重大突破，对AI基础设施竞争格局有重要影响。
