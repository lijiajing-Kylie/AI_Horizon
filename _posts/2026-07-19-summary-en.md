## [Trump admin considers FINRA-like watchdog for top AI models](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-17/us-considers-creating-finra-like-watchdog-to-vet-top-ai-models) ⭐️ 8.0/10

The Trump administration is considering creating an independent AI regulator modeled after FINRA to vet top AI models for safety. Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent is leading the initiative, and the plan is under review by White House Chief of Staff Susie Wiles. This would create a permanent, industry-funded oversight body, replacing ad hoc government controls and giving Wall Street and Silicon Valley more say in safety standards. It directly affects major AI labs like OpenAI and Anthropic, which have clashed with the government over model releases. The proposed body would report to the SEC, similar to FINRA's relationship with the agency. The plan aligns with a recent suggestion by Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis, but President Trump has not yet reviewed it and the framework remains subject to change.
> **Reason**: 特朗普政府拟设立类似FINRA的独立机构审查顶尖AI模型，这一政策动向将直接影响AI产业的监管格局，涉及OpenAI、Anthropic等主要公司，具有重大现实影响和公共讨论价值。

## [San Francisco orders Apple and Google to remove AI nudify apps](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/17/apple-and-google-ordered-to-purge-nudify-apps-from-app-stores/) ⭐️ 8.0/10

San Francisco City Attorney David Chiu has ordered Apple and Google to remove dozens of 'nudify' apps from their app stores, citing violations of California laws against non-consensual deepfake pornography. This marks the first time a city government has directly demanded platform action against AI-powered nudify apps, potentially setting a precedent for local enforcement of deepfake regulations. It pressures Apple and Google to take more proactive responsibility for content moderation and payment processing. The city claims Apple and Google have made millions in fees from these apps and have been on notice for nearly a year. Both companies have removed or suspended some apps but the city demands broader action within 28 days or face civil penalties.
> **Reason**: 这是首次有城市政府直接要求苹果和谷歌下架利用AI生成深度伪造色情内容的应用，涉及AI监管、平台责任和社会影响，具有重要政策意义。
